About

Welcome to Ryan Li's stock site! This site allows you to track and manage your stock portfolio with ease.

Here are some key functionalities:

Home Page

  • Search for stock information using the ticker symbol.
  • View detailed stock data including current price, open price, high price, low price, 52-week high, 52-week low, and market capitalization.

Saved Stocks

  • Add stocks to your portfolio and track their performance.
  • Delete stocks from your portfolio when you no longer want to track them.

Note: If you see IBM data or repeated data, it is most likely because I ran out of API calls for the day.

Note: Stock information is gathered from the previous day's closing prices as real-time updates cost money.
